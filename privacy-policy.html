Privacy Policy

Islamic Dream Interpretation (Ta'beer), Duas, and Zikr Mobile Application

Effective Date:

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Islamic Dream Interpretation (Ta'beer), Duas, and Zikr mobile application ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

This App is developed and owned by Umair Anwari and is designed to provide Islamic content including dream interpretations, daily duas, zikr, and other Islamic educational materials in Farsi (primary), Pashto, and Urdu languages.

Important Note: We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your information. This App does not collect personal information from users.

2. Information Collection and Use

Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • User account credentials or registration data
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Photos, videos, or personal media files
  • Contacts or social media information
  • Financial or payment information

Information That May Be Automatically Collected:

  • Basic app usage analytics (completely anonymous)
  • Crash reports and error logs (no personal data included)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and language preferences

How We Use Information: The limited anonymous data we may collect is used solely to:

  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Understand which features are most used
  • Ensure compatibility across different devices

3. Log Data

Like many mobile applications, our App may collect information automatically through your device's standard logging mechanisms. This information may include:

  • App crash reports
  • Performance metrics
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Device technical specifications

All log data is completely anonymous and cannot be used to identify individual users. We use this information solely for technical support and app improvement purposes.

4. Cookies

Our App does not use cookies. Since this is a mobile application with primarily offline content, we do not implement cookie technology for tracking or storing user information.

However, if the App integrates with third-party services (such as Google Play Services), those services may use their own tracking mechanisms according to their respective privacy policies.

5. Service Providers

We may employ third-party companies and services to support our App's functionality, including:

  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and basic device integration
  • AdMob (if applicable): For displaying advertisements within the App
  • Analytics Services: For anonymous usage statistics and app performance monitoring
  • Content Delivery Networks: For efficient content delivery

Important: These third-party service providers have access to your information only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

Each third-party service provider operates under their own privacy policy, which we recommend you review.

6. Security

We prioritize the security of our App and your information. While we do not collect personal information, we implement appropriate security measures to protect:

  • App content and Islamic materials
  • User preferences and settings
  • Anonymous usage data

Our security measures include:

  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Secure content delivery methods
  • Protection against unauthorized access
  • Compliance with mobile security best practices

However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Children's Information

Our Islamic App is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for users of all ages, including children.

Our Commitment: We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

The App's content is appropriate for general audiences and aligns with Islamic values and teachings.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy within the App
  • Updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy
  • Sending app notifications (if technically feasible)

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes become effective when posted on this page.

9. Your Rights and Choices

Since we do not collect personal information, you have minimal data to manage. However, you have the right to:

  • Uninstall the App at any time
  • Clear app data and cache through your device settings
  • Opt out of analytics through device settings (if available)
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

10. International Users

Our App is available internationally and is designed to serve Muslim communities worldwide. The content is primarily in Farsi, with support for Pashto and Urdu languages.

If you are accessing the App from outside the country where it is developed, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries where our service providers operate.
